About me

Hi, I'm Helen. I started out working in a variety of industries - from the art and illustration world to global design and marketing agencies. In every case, I always found myself drawn to how good web design and development can better connect people on common interests and create opportunities for collaboration.

Becoming a software engineer has let me work more directly with people who bring a wide range of perspectives and expertise to build solutions that work both efficiently and beautifully. For me, one of the most rewarding parts is getting to immediately put new learnings into practice every day.

Find me in NYC (for most days of the year) or online below.
